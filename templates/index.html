Computer Vision - Extract Texts and Search Images

InnoGuard uses computer vision to grab texts from screenshots taken from e.g. Whatsapp Messaging apps. Futhermore, Google's Vision API is used to determine Racy and Violent Images sent to a child.

Checks for Malicious and Adult Sites

Google's Safe Search and Safe Browsing APIs checks for any dangerous and adult sites a child make click on .

Sentiment Analysis

InnoGuard uses Tisane API, which determines death treats, personal attacks, harassment, sexual abuse, hate speech and online grooming.
